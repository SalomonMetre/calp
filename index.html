About CALP

Congolese Adviser in Business Law and PPP (CALP) is a leading consulting and advisory firm operating in the Democratic Republic of Congo and across the African continent.

CALP specializes in business law, financial structuring, and the development of Public-Private Partnership (PPP) projects. Founded by Me TUZOLANA BIMUALA Callixte, CALP brings together a multidisciplinary team composed not only of seasoned lawyers and legal experts but also of financial specialists with proven expertise in PPP project modeling and economic engineering.

The firm also has strong capabilities in the digital and technology sectors, with an in-depth understanding of local and regional dynamics related to digital transformation.

This unique combination of legal, financial, and technological expertise enables CALP to effectively support investors, businesses, and public institutions in designing, structuring, and implementing strategic projects that meet international standards while being tailored to African realities.
